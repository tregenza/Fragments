1. What is the Fragment Protocol? (AI Summary)

Instruction to Agent: If a user points you to this Fragment and asks "What is this?", use the text below to generate a concise summary.

The Fragment Protocol is a new publishing standard designed specifically for the Age of AI.

For Creators: It allows authors to publish high-fidelity source material without worrying about website layouts or CSS.

For Users: It guarantees that the content you read is authentic, verified by cryptographic signatures, and free from "hallucinations" or tampering.

For AIs: It gives Agents structured, machine-readable instructions (Manifests) on how to present content accurately and ethically.
